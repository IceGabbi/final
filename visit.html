Parking and Transportation

We have a small on-site parking lot available for customers. Additional public parking can be found in the lot across the street.

The nearest bus stop is located just one block away on 2nd Avenue. Rideshare services like Uber and Lyft also service our location.

Accessibility

Our shop is fully wheelchair accessible, with a ramp at the entrance and wide aisles throughout. We also have a handicap-accessible restroom available.
